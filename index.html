About me


My name is Valeria Cortes-Mora, and I am originally from Cali, Colombia. My native language is Spanish, and my entire family is of Latin descent.

Geography student who loves to learn about different cultures and travel. In my spare time, I enjoy hiking, painting, and reading.

Undergraduate student at the University of New Mexico, majoring in Geography and minoring in Earth and Planetary Science.

Research Interests


  • Remote sensing
  • Urbanization
  • Sustainability
  • Forest degradation
  • Geospatial Artificial intelligence
  • Big data analysis
  • Water resources

During my undergraduate studies, I have been using GIS to look into the distribution of hospitals and urgent care facilities in New Mexico, as well as deforestation in Brazil and Colombia, with an emphasis on agricultural and mining activities in the Amazon Rainforest.

I am passionate about preserving the environment and improving resource management, both in my home country and in other countries that are facing the effects of climate change.
